Cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger is a hamburger with a slice of cheese on top of the patty, typically served on a bun and originating from the United States. It is a hot main course made from a ground beef patty, cheese, and a bun, with variations in ingredients and composition. Common additions include condiments like ketchup, lettuce, tomato, and onion.

Ingredients

  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Bun
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Form the ground meat into patties, season, and cook them until done. The cheese is typically added near the end of cooking to melt.
  2. Toast the inside of the buns for added texture.
  3. Place the cheese-topped patty on the bottom bun, then add any desired toppings and sauces before placing the top bun on.
