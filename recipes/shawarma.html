Shawarma

Description

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern rotisserie dish made from seasoned meat, like lamb, beef, or chicken, stacked on a vertical spit and slow-roasted. The cooked meat is shaved into thin slices and typically served in a flatbread like pita, often with vegetables, pickles, and sauces such as tahini or garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Wrap
  • Fillings
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Thinly sliced meat is marinated and stacked on a vertical spit in a cone shape.
  2. The spit rotates slowly, and the meat is roasted on a vertical rotisserie or in an oven.
  3. Thin layers of meat are shaved off the spit with a sharp knife.
